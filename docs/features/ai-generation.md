# AI Title & Keywords

SendStock AI uses vision AI models to analyze your images and videos and generate optimized metadata for stock platforms.

## How It Works

1. Your image is resized locally (max 512px) before being sent
2. The AI analyzes the visual content and context
3. It generates a title and up to 50 keywords
4. Results are pre-filled in the editor — you review and confirm

::: info Privacy
Images are resized and sent to the AI model for analysis. No files are stored on our servers.
:::

## Single File Generation

Click the **AI** button on any file card to generate metadata for that file.

## Batch Generation

Click **Generate All** in the toolbar to process all loaded files in sequence. You can monitor progress and cancel at any time.

## Customizing the AI

Go to **Profile → My Prompt** to add a custom instruction to guide the AI:

- Specify your style: `"Always include lighting conditions"`
- Target a niche: `"Focus on business and corporate context"`
- Set keyword style: `"Use simple, common English words"`

## Editing Results

All generated titles and keywords are fully editable:

- Click on the title to edit inline
- Click on a keyword to remove it
- Type in the keywords field to add new ones
