# Portfolio Sync

Import and analyze your Adobe Stock portfolio directly inside SendStock AI.

## What is Portfolio Sync?

Portfolio Sync connects to your Adobe Stock contributor account and imports your entire catalog — titles, keywords, download counts, and thumbnails — into a local database.

This lets you:
- Browse your full portfolio offline
- Identify your best-performing assets
- Find gaps in your keyword coverage
- Detect duplicate or underperforming content

## How to Set Up

1. Go to **Settings → Adobe Stock**
2. Enter your **Adobe Stock Creator ID** (found in your contributor dashboard)
3. Click **Sync Portfolio**

The initial sync may take a few minutes depending on portfolio size.

## Auto-Sync

Once configured, SendStock AI automatically syncs your portfolio on startup if a Creator ID is detected.

## Portfolio Analytics

After syncing, the **Portfolio** tab shows:

- Total assets and download stats
- Best-selling files
- Keyword frequency analysis
- Assets with no downloads (candidates for re-keywording or deletion)
