# Getting Started — Desktop App

The SendStock AI desktop app gives you the full feature set, including tools that run directly on your machine.

## Download & Install

Download from [sendstockai.com](https://www.sendstockai.com).

**Windows** — Microsoft Store (recommended) or `.exe` installer

**macOS** — `.dmg` installer

## Sign In

Open the app and sign in with your SendStock AI account.

## What You Can Do

- Everything available in the Web App, plus:
- Upload directly to stock platforms via FTP/SFTP
- Sync and analyze your portfolio
- Detect duplicate or similar assets
- Upscale images
- Remove backgrounds
- Use a local AI model (no credits consumed)

## First Steps

1. Sign in to your account
2. Drag and drop files onto the main window
3. Click **Generate AI** to generate titles and keywords
4. Configure your upload servers in **Settings → Platforms**
5. Click **Save & Upload** to upload directly

## Auto-Update

The app updates automatically. When a new version is available, you will see a notification inside the app.
