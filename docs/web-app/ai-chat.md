# AI Portfolio Chat — Web App

Chat with an AI expert about your Adobe Stock portfolio — get performance analysis, market insights, competitor research, and optimized metadata in a conversational interface.

::: info Pro feature
AI Portfolio Chat is available on paid plans only.
:::

---

## First-Time Setup

Before you can chat, you need to connect your Adobe Stock portfolio:

1. Go to the **Chat** tab in the web app
2. Enter your **Adobe Stock Contributor ID** (find it at contributor.stock.adobe.com → your profile)
3. Click **Save** — your portfolio syncs automatically (progress shown in a modal)
4. Once the sync is complete, the chat is ready

---

## Interface Overview

The chat screen has two panels side by side:

- **Left — Chat**: Conversation with the AI
- **Right — Portfolio Browser**: Browse and attach your assets to the conversation

---

## What the AI Can Do

### Portfolio Analysis

- Overview of your portfolio: total assets, breakdown by type (photos, illustrations, vectors, videos)
- Best-selling assets and your top-performing keywords
- Assets with zero downloads — optimization candidates
- Download trends over 12 months

### Market & Competitor Research

- Search the Adobe Stock marketplace for any niche or keyword
- Identify trending keywords by category
- Analyze any public creator's top 100 assets (enter their Contributor ID)
- Explore the full Adobe Stock category tree

### Metadata Generation

When you ask the AI to generate a title, keywords, or description, it outputs a **metadata card** directly in the chat — ready to copy:

```text
Title         [copy]
Keywords      [copy]   word · word · word · ...
Description   [copy]
```

### Image Analysis

Upload an image in the chat and the AI will:

- Evaluate its stock potential
- Suggest a title, keywords, and description
- Identify the content and composition

Accepted formats: `JPG` `PNG` `SVG` `EPS`

---

## Portfolio Context Toggle

The chat input has a **"My Adobe Portfolio"** toggle button:

| State | Behavior |
| --- | --- |
| **ON** (green) | AI automatically uses your portfolio data to ground its recommendations |
| **OFF** | AI uses only market and category data — no access to your assets |

Turn it **ON** when asking about your own performance. Turn it **OFF** for general market research.

---

## Attaching Assets from Your Portfolio

In the right panel, click any asset to **attach it to your next message**. The AI receives the asset's title, current keywords, download count, and category — and gives you targeted advice for that specific file.

Portfolio panel controls:

- 5 tabs: All / Photos / Illustrations / Vectors / Videos
- Search by title or keyword
- Sort by date or downloads
- 50 assets per page
- **Refresh** button to force a re-sync from Adobe Stock

---

## Example Prompts

- *"Give me an overview of my portfolio performance"*
- *"What are my best-selling categories?"*
- *"Find a profitable niche with low competition"*
- *"Analyze the top creators in the 'business' category"*
- *"Generate metadata for this photo"* (attach image)
- *"Why is this asset not selling?"* (attach asset from right panel)
- *"What keywords should I add to increase downloads?"*

---

## Tips

- After each response, the AI suggests **2–4 follow-up questions** — click one to continue the conversation
- The AI detects your language automatically (English, French, Spanish, German)
- The conversation persists during your browser session — refreshing the tab keeps your history
