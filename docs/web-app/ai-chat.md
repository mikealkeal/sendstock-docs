# AI Portfolio Chat — Web App

Chat with an AI expert about your portfolio — get analysis, market insights, and optimized metadata in a conversational interface.

## What You Can Do

- Ask questions about your portfolio performance
- Get keyword and title suggestions for your files
- Analyze your best-selling and underperforming assets
- Explore market trends and competitor strategies
- Share an image and get an instant stock potential analysis

## How to Use

1. Go to the **Chat** tab in the web app
2. Type your question or request
3. The AI responds with insights based on your real portfolio data

**Examples:**

- *"What are my best-selling categories?"*
- *"Suggest keywords for this photo"* (attach an image)
- *"What trends should I focus on this month?"*
- *"Why is this asset underperforming?"*
