# Plans & Credits

## How Credits Work

Credits are consumed each time you generate a title, keywords, or description for a file.

Without a subscription, you have **no credits**. You need to subscribe or purchase credits to use AI generation.

## Plans

Visit [sendstockai.com](https://www.sendstockai.com) for current pricing and plan details.

## Checking Your Balance

Your remaining credits are displayed in the top bar of the application.

## What Happens When Credits Run Out?

When your daily credits are exhausted, AI generation is paused until the next reset. You can still:
- Edit existing metadata manually
- Upload files already processed
- Export CSV files

## Subscription Management

Manage your subscription from **Profile → Subscription** inside the app or from your account page on [sendstockai.com](https://www.sendstockai.com).
