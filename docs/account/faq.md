# FAQ

## General

### What platforms does SendStock AI support?

Currently: **Adobe Stock**, **Shutterstock**, and **Freepik**. More platforms are planned.

### Is my data safe?

- Files are **never stored** on our servers
- For AI generation, images are resized (max 512px) before being sent to the AI model
- FTP credentials are stored **encrypted** on your device
- Portfolio data is stored in a **local database** on your machine

### Does SendStock AI work offline?

Partially. You can load files, edit metadata, and use similarity search offline. AI generation and upload require an internet connection.

---

## AI Generation

### Why are the keywords sometimes irrelevant?

The AI is trained on general visual content. For niche or very specific subjects, add a custom prompt in **Profile → My Prompt** to guide the AI toward your specialty.

### Can I use my own AI model?

Yes, advanced users can configure a local AI model (LMStudio, Ollama) in their account settings via the web app.

## Upload & FTP

### My FTP connection fails — what should I do?

1. Double-check credentials in your contributor dashboard
2. Make sure you're not behind a restrictive firewall
3. Try the **Test Connection** button to see the exact error message

### Can I upload to multiple platforms at the same time?

Yes. Select your files, click **Upload**, and check all the platforms you want to upload to simultaneously.

## Account

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Profile → Subscription → Cancel** inside the app, or contact support at [sendstockai.com](https://www.sendstockai.com).

### I lost my credits — what happened?

Credits reset daily at midnight Paris time. If you believe there was an error, contact support with the date and time of the issue.
