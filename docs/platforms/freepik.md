# Freepik

## Requirements

- An active **Freepik Contributor** account
- SFTP credentials from your Freepik contributor dashboard

## Getting SFTP Credentials

1. Log in to your Freepik contributor account
2. Navigate to the upload section
3. Request or retrieve your SFTP credentials

## Configuring in SendStock AI

1. Open **Settings → Platforms → Freepik**
2. Enter your SFTP credentials
3. Click **Test Connection**

## Upload Notes

- Freepik uses **SFTP** (not FTP)
- Vectors: `.ai` or `.eps` with a JPEG preview
- Photos: high-resolution JPEG
- Keywords: up to **30 tags**
