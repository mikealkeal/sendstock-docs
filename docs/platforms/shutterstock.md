# Shutterstock

## Requirements

- An active **Shutterstock Contributor** account
- FTP credentials from your Shutterstock contributor dashboard

## Getting FTP Credentials

1. Log in to [submit.shutterstock.com](https://submit.shutterstock.com)
2. Go to **Upload → FTP**
3. Note your FTP host and credentials

## Configuring in SendStock AI

1. Open **Settings → Platforms → Shutterstock**
2. Enter your FTP credentials
3. Click **Test Connection**

## Upload Notes

- Photos: minimum **4 megapixels**, JPEG format recommended
- Videos: minimum **1920×1080**, H.264 codec
- Keywords: maximum **50 keywords** per asset
- Title: maximum **200 characters**
