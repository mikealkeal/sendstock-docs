# Adobe Stock

## Requirements

- An active **Adobe Stock Contributor** account
- FTP credentials from your Adobe Stock contributor dashboard

## Getting FTP Credentials

1. Log in to [contributor.stock.adobe.com](https://contributor.stock.adobe.com)
2. Go to **Upload → FTP Upload**
3. Copy your FTP host, username, and password

## Configuring in SendStock AI

1. Open **Settings → Platforms → Adobe Stock**
2. Enter your FTP credentials
3. Click **Test Connection**

## Upload Notes

- Adobe Stock requires files to be **at least 4 megapixels** for photos
- Videos must be **at least 1080p**
- Keywords: maximum **50 keywords** per asset
- Title: maximum **200 characters**

## Portfolio Sync

SendStock AI can sync your Adobe Stock portfolio to analyze performance. See [Portfolio Sync](/features/portfolio-sync).
