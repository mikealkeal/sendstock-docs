# Similarity Search — Desktop App

Detect duplicate or visually similar assets before uploading — entirely on your device.

## How It Works

SendStock AI computes a visual fingerprint for each image locally. Fingerprints are compared to find assets that look similar, even if the files are different.

**Everything runs on your machine** — no images are sent to any server for this feature.

## How to Use

1. Go to the **Similarity** tab
2. Drop an image or select one from your portfolio
3. SendStock AI scans three sources simultaneously:
   - Your **synced portfolio** (imported assets)
   - Your **current session** (files loaded in the app)
   - A **live search** against the broader catalog
4. Results appear ranked by similarity with a risk level

## Risk Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Low | Visually different — safe to upload |
| Medium | Some resemblance — review recommended |
| High | Very similar — consider revising before uploading |
| Cluster | 4 or more similar assets detected in your portfolio |

## Why Use It

Stock platforms penalize or reject duplicate submissions. Running a similarity check before uploading helps you:

- Avoid duplicate rejections
- Identify series of similar shots
- Keep your portfolio clean and varied
