# Portfolio Sync — Desktop App

Import your contributor portfolio into SendStock AI to analyze performance and find opportunities.

## What Portfolio Sync Does

- Imports your full catalog (titles, keywords, thumbnails, status)
- Shows download counts and earnings per asset
- Identifies best-selling and underperforming files
- Enables local similarity search across your entire portfolio

## Setup

1. Go to **Settings → Portfolio**
2. Enter your **Creator ID** (found in your contributor dashboard)
3. Click **Sync Portfolio**

The first sync may take a few minutes depending on your portfolio size.

## Auto-Sync

Once configured, SendStock AI automatically syncs your portfolio silently at startup.

## What You Can See

After syncing, the **Portfolio** tab shows:

- Your full asset catalog with thumbnails
- Status per file: draft, approved, rejected
- Download count per file
- Earnings (when available)
- Pagination — 50 assets per page

## Filtering

Filter your portfolio by status to focus on specific subsets:

- **All** — full catalog
- **Approved** — live assets
- **Rejected** — assets to review or resubmit
- **Draft** — pending review
